Pasta

Italian pasta (pronounced “PAS-tah”) is a collective name for a category of food made
from wheat flour and water, sometimes with egg. The name refers to the resulting dough (pasta also means “dough”)
that is rolled out and cut into various shapes. Pasta is cooked in water and served with a sauce.

Rice

Rice is the seed of a semi-aquatic grass (Oryza sativa)
that is cultivated extensively in warm climates in
many countries, including the United States, for its edible
grain. It is a staple food throughout the world.
